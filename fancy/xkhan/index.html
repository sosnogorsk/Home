Songs included "Mother of All Battles" and "It's Never Too Late To Riot."

We would have video projections and subject audience members to ..nevermind.. It may still happen.

We would also have a bunch of touring cheerleaders. Dieu would have been one of our cheerleaders. She just really wanted to wear a costume.

T-shirt design. Of course. An image of Genghis Khan and the unholy Mongolian horde descending upon the United States.
