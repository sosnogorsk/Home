I have been obsessed with food books for a while. I will speak briefly about two books in particular--Win Her With Dinner and The Futurist Cookbook. I simply adore The Futurist Cookbook. I hate the other book. This book is simply a play on concepts from both books. It's the realization that food is a very visual object and that food really does set the mood.

This book was done in collaboration with Megan Purn. She thought up of the idea to write a book of how to make food for your enemies. Not necessarily food to poison them, but food to confuse your enemies. The quotes are from The Art of War.
