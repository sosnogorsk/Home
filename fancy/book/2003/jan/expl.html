I saw this amazing book at a Fluxus show for Dick Higgins at Otis Art School. It was an amazing book called 256 Little Clouds by Dieter Roth. In the book, Roth put these scraps of paper onto a sheet of paper and photographed the page in raking light. This created a shadow that made you want to pick up the sheet of paper (or cloud). This is my attempt to recreate that feeling of want to touch a page.
