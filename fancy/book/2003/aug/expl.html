This book is a reaction towards this perfect freeway that was built in Georgia. This freeway was going to last forever. On opening day, plants had already started to grow and began to crack the concrete.

I dreamed of plants taking revenge on motorists. As I drive to and from work, a plant in its anger picks me up and puts me in a tree.

I got really lazy and decided to collect pictures driving up and down teh 101 and sketch them.
