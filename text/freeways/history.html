Freeway Interchange Institute - History
The genesis for this group started with a crash and a boom. A member was in a near death collision at the Kellogg Interchange in the Inland Empire. This accident would have been avoidable with clear directions, better signage and simpler interchanges.

Since that storied beginning, the Freeway Interchange Institute has followed the pathway of giving better information to drivers and to road-builders and city-planners.
