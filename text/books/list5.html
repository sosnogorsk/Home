DCMARCH2004
In March 2004, I decided to make a book commemorating my trip to DC. I worked with Adam Graham-Silverman on a book that used unconventional methods of reading. We were influenced by John Barth's Lost in the Funhouse.

Guardian Thug
Someone reminded me of a book that I made in 1998 called Guardian Thug. (Yes, making books is an ongoing mode of expression.) I have no recollection of the content of this book and opted to return to the topic. This time, the thug again does no violence, but the threat hangs heavily in the background. I was influenced by Jan Pienkowski's Easter.

Garden Slug
