Chicken
In September 2003, the Uncle Justin Series introduces the poetic yearnings of a chicken. Somehow, the text turns out as pure gibberish; it may be the impure product that chickens are fed. (40/40)

hurry
A piece of propaganda tucked into a gum wrapper. What could this signify? October 2003's book questions the quick process of progress and makes the inspired claim of a new modern sin. HURRY! (40/40)

mango (pt. 2)
The metamorphosis of a lovely lass to a ripe mango is detailed in luscious watercolor. The beautiful November 2003 book is a memorable keeper. (40/40)

Peace Worm
In this modern society, even worms have a message. A charming 2003 greeting that encourages healty eating and sharing. This kinetic structure is made of red poster board, lime green Fabriano paper and brass fasteners. (70/70)

MIGHT
With imagery made from Stonehenge black paper and a white crayon, the January 2003 book explores with acerbic wit ideas of equality. (40/40)
