BOOK LIST

Move
This book was about the mixed feelings that follow a move. Paper was Sommerset Satin White and the book was hand-bound in signatures with plum-colored Japanese bookcloth davey board. (1/1) Book was lost in the mail.

Scared Cactus
This was September 2002's book. It is about a molested cactus. The book is made of photocopied drawings on letter paper with a staple as binding. There is a song and a fun activity included. (30/30) There are a three copies available still.

Cactus
This was October 2002's book. It is about a molested cactus and its relationship with progress. The book is an accordian fold with corrugated cardboard covers. (30/30) There is one copy left.

Mitochondria
November 2002 brings us a tour de force. The book has multitudes of mitochondria revealing the truth of their importance. This book was constructed out of photocopied art on letter paper with green yarn pamphlet stitch binding. (30/30) There are two copies left.
